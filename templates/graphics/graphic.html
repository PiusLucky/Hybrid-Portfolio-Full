Design Process

Most of the conventions I follow upon any new projects are as follows:
  • Providing a Creative Brief for Clients Using Jotform
  • Indulging in extensive research as regards competitors design [providing better quality!]
  • Moodboarding, Brainstorming and Sketching
  • Concepts and Concepts Iteration
  • Following Graphic Design Production Process and Refining of Design
  • Client Approval Process
  • Final design delivery
